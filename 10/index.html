Wait 'n Wail

  • High Concept: Fight against Eddie, the Mean old Yeti in this battle between video game dimensions!
  • Theme: State which of the Storymatic themes you used.
  • Mandated Variety: Random movement of the Yeti.
  • Player Experience Goals: What experience do you want players to have when playing your game?
  • Gameplay:

    Controls: Arrow keys - navigate menu/move, Z - Confirm options(main menu/turn-based mode)/attack(in action mode), X - Guard (in action mode)

    Attack the yeti to reduce its health. Defending will reduce the damage you take by half.

    Bring the yeti's health to 0 to win. (Win screen/further stages/enemies not yet implemented)

  • Prototype Goal: Tests integration between common staic player positions in turn-based play vs fast paced movement in action games.
  • Features: Music changes on switching modes from the same point, rather than starting from the beginning.
  • Setting/Premise: Colliseum fight.
  • Story: Eddie, the Mean old Yeti, refuses to allow Exong to return home. As such he is challenged to fight for his freedom in the colliseum.
  • Target Audience: Those into action and strategy.
  • Play Time: ~5 minutes
