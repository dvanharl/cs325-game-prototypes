Ephemeral Maelstrom

  • High Concept: A fast-paced rush to destroy the incoming asteroid to stop it from destroying the earth!
  • Theme: "Cowboy" & "Asteroid falling to earth"
  • Mandated Variety: Mouse position/click input implemented.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want players to feel powerful playing the space cowboy as they blow through cult members and the massive meteor to pieces. They can also try to get the top score.
  • Gameplay: Players have one minute to stop the meteor from crashing into the earth! Use the mouse to shoot your gun. Shoot at the meteor to chip away at it until it breaks and win! However, the cult members will appear, trying to shoot at you. Shoot them or else you will lose a health point. Lose all of them and it's game over! Points: Hitting Meteor - 100pts, Killing Enemy - 200pts, Destroying Meteor - 10,000 pts
  • Prototype Goal: This game tests quick reflexes and clicking skill to quell the enemies appearing while taking care of the meteor. The later game would include dynamic timers and shaking effects to better show the meteor's approach, as well as a health bar for the meteor, but due to time constraints, this couldn't be met.
  • Features: Custom GUI and animations! Go for the high score!

  • Story: A meteor is hurtling towards the earth! It's too close for any normal weaponry to divert it. Luckily, Esgau, the Space Cowboy, stopped on his way to find a space companion and came across this predicament. With his ion arm cannon, he surely has enough power to rid of it! Unfortunately, local cults pining for the end of days have heard of his plans to stop the meteor, and in turn plan to stop him! Will Esgau save the earth, or will it shatter into pieces, drifting into the unknown abyss?
  • Target Audience: Players looking for quick, on-the-draw, action.
  • Play Time: Each round will take at most 1 minute at which point the time limit will run out.
  • Strategies: Focus on the meteor but try to take care of enemies immediately as they appear.
