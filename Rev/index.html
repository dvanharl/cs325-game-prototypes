Crenae Arco

  • High Concept: A deep seated quest as an undead, battle to punish the hordes of zombies that turned you this way!
  • Theme: Revenge
  • Mandated Variety: Random enemy spawn times.
  • Player Experience Goals: What experience do you want players to have when playing your game?
  • Gameplay: Z: Jump X: Fire Left/Right Arrow keys: Move Left/Right Fight off the ghosts until the dawn! If the ghosts touch you, you'll lose a health point. Reduce your health to 0, and it's game over!
  • Prototype Goal: What game mechanic is this prototype evaluating?
  • Features: What are the unique aspects of your game that set it apart?
  • Setting/Premise: A forest in France, abound with the souls of the undead.
  • Story: A simple girl bursting with vivacity, her life was cut short on a simple errand through the forest due to a tragic run in with the undead. Although now one of them, she rejects their ways, fighting until dawn when the ghosts - completely vulnerable to the sun - vanish, so that she may return back home.
